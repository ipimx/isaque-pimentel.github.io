Isaque Pimentel

Ph.D. student

Ecole Polytechnique

My name is Isaque Pimentel and I am currently a Ph.D. student at École Polytechnique. My main research interests are Stochastic Control and Monte Carlo methods applied to Mathematical Finance.

I am a candidate to a Ph.D in Applied Mathematics from the Centre de Mathématiques Appliquées (CMAP). I am sponsored by CIFRE contract with EDF R&D at Optimisation Simulation Risk and Statistics (OSIRIS) department. My advisor is Professor Emmanuel Gobet, coordinator of the SIMPAS research team.

During my M.Sc., I did an internship at EDF Lab in the OSIRIS department and another at BNP Paribas in the Global Equities and Commodities Derivative (GECD) team. I earned a M.Sc. in Applied mathematics at École Polytechnique and M.Sc. in Probability and Finance at Université Paris Diderot. Additionally, I studied for a B.Eng in Electronic at ITA in Brazil.
